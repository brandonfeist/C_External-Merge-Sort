Abstract— The QAR (Quick Access Recorder) records 
immense amounts of aircraft flight data which reflect the 
performance of every parts of aircraft. This paper 
describes the importance of analyzing QAR data in flight 
safety. We proposed a novel data-cube based method to 
process a large number of QAR data and extracts 
association rules about the parameters of aircraft engine 
which is useful for engine diagnostics and health 
management. 
 
Key words-QAR; aircraft safty;data mining; association 
rules;data cube
